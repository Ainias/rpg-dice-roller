Accepted formats:

  • 6 (A 6 sided die)
  • d6 (A 6 sided die)
  • 2d6 (Two 6 sided dice)
  • 2d10+4+1d20 (Roll two 10 sided dice add four, and roll one 20 sided die)
  • 2d10+4+2d20-L (Roll two 10 sided dice add four, and roll two 20 sided die, taking away the lowest of the two)
